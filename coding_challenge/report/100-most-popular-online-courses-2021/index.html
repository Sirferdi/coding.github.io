The 100 Most Popular सप्ताहनिशुल्क ऑनालइन Courses (2021 Edition)

This year, the ranking looks a bit different: almost one fifth of the courses are related to the pandemic.

Suparn Patra Nov 30th, 2020
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Envelope
  • 4

It’s been nine years since a few स्टैनफोर्ड professors decided to offer their courses online के लिएfree. These later became known as MOOCs, के लिएMassive Open Online Courses. And 2012 became the ‘Year of the MOOC’.

Well, 2020 might be MOOCs’ next most consequential year. The COVID-19 pandemic has boosted learners’ interest in online education. MOOC providers, in particular, have seen a significant traffic influx, as previously covered by Class Central’s Report.

The pandemic has also impacted the most popular list. Compared to last year’s 100 most popular courses, the number of health and medicine online courses has gone up.

In fact, 18 courses in the ranking are directly related to COVID. The top course, with over 1 million enrollments, is Johns Hopkins’ COVID-19 Contact Tracing. It is followed by Harvard’s COVID-19 के लिए मैकेनिकल वेंटिलेशन, with 300K enrollments.

The courses at the bottom of the ranking hover around 15K enrollments. Combined, these 100 सप्ताहनिशुल्क ऑनालइन courses account के लिए6 million enrollments.

We’re happy to see that the first course we took as a study group at Class Central, Macquarie’s Excel Fundamentals के लिएData Analysis, also made the ranking. Read about our experience here.

Methodology

First, I went through Class Central’s database and made a list of all the courses that were offered के लिएthe first time ever in 2020. This was around 2800 courses.

Then I wrote scrapers to collect enrollment numbers across four different providers: Coursera, edX, FutureLearn, and स्वयं. They all show how many students are enrolled on their course pages.

Combined, these providers have garnered over 11 million enrollments के लिएcourses launched in 2020. The 100 most popular courses account के लिएover half of these enrollments.
